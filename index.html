My Services

The Run

A group 5K/10K suitable for all paces through an iconic area of the city (or coast), with an additional path guide for those that want more mileage or additional runs. You'll also receive a medal and t-shirt!

The Meals

There’s no better way to start a trip then with good food. You’ll always get a welcome dinner on our trips and a post-run breakfast on day 2. Everything else is covered by you.

Accommodations

Two nights in the middle of the city’s major attractions. We’ll also provide a city guide and recommendations for things to get into while you’re there

Evening Social

We end night two by building community and sharing laughs at a comedy show in La Jolla!

Weekend Itinerary

First Day 17 April 2020 Second Day 18 April 2020 Third Day 19 April 2020

Arrival & Check In

4:30 - 8 pm

Meet us at our accommodations to check-in and unload. You'll unwind and meet some of our other travellers once we meet in the lobby to head to dinner. Transportation will be provided to and from dinner for those that want to return immediately after.

We will provide city-guides to all guests prior to dinner for you to make out plans that work for your interests.

Morning Run & Breakfast

07:00 am - 10:30 am

We'll send a general wake up call at 7AM for us to leave to our run site at 7:30AM. At the site, we'll share the virtual maps with everyone based on their distances and break up into groups with our run guides. Upon meeting at the finish, we'll stretch and head to breakfast. You should be back to your rooms by 10:30AM to freshen up and take on the day.

Enjoy the City

Noon - 7:30 pm

This is your time to enjoy Sunny San Diego! We recommend the Zoo + Safari, but we're also not above taking a stroll around Mission Bay, lunch in Little Italy or Bar Hopping in the Gaslamp District. The city is your oyster. Let us know if you need suggestions.

The Comedy Store @ La Jolla

09:00 pm - Midnight

This event is completely optional! Please elect whether or not you'd like to join us at sign-up! We'll meet at the store at 9PM for a show that starts at 9:30. Food and drinks are served at the comedy club for those that are interested. You can visit their website for more information regarding their lineup. We will provide transportation from the comedy club back to your room.

Wake Up Call & Breakfast

08:00 am - 09:00am

We'll provide a light breakfast prior to check out for our travellers. Check out will begin at 10AM. If you would like to arrange an airport or train transfer, please let us know. We'd be happy to help accomodate!

About Us

Runcation is all about a weekend away and a peaceful run through a brand new route. Created by a woman that loves traveling, especially exploring by foot, a Runcation is the perfect vacation for those trying to get out while maintaining a healthy lifestyle.

These trips are ideal for new runners still that want to engage in short-middle distance runs.

Everyone is welcome - trips are tailored to participants. 

Our trip format is consistent - welcome dinner, morning run (whether around the mouments, down the coast, etc), a light yoga session, breakfast and and a social event. Your overnight accomodations are always included in the price.
